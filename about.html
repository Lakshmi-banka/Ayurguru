About Us


At Ayurguru, we are committed to helping individuals understand their unique Ayurvedic constitution, or prakriti. Our mission is to provide personalized dietary and lifestyle recommendations that promote balance and harmony in everyday life.

We believe that everyone is unique, and by understanding your dosha type, you can make informed choices about your health and wellness. Our team of Ayurvedic experts utilizes traditional knowledge combined with modern insights to empower you on your wellness journey.

Our services include personalized consultations, dietary plans, and wellness workshops. Whether you are looking to improve your overall health, manage stress, or simply learn more about Ayurveda, Ayurguru is here to guide you every step of the way.

Join us in exploring the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda and discover how it can enhance your life today!
