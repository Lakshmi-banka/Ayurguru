Understanding Kapha Prakriti


Kapha is the energy associated with structure and lubrication and is one of the three fundamental energies (or doshas) in Ayurveda. Here are some key characteristics and recommendations for individuals with a dominant Kapha dosha:

Characteristics:

  • Solid and sturdy build
  • Good stamina
  • A calm, steady disposition

Common Issues:

  • Prone to weight gain
  • Congestion
  • Lethargy when out of balance

Digestive Patterns:

  • Slow metabolism and tendency toward water retention

Diet Recommendations:

  • Light, warm, and stimulating foods
  • Avoid heavy and oily foods
  • Include plenty of spices

Balance Tips:

  • Engage in regular exercise
  • Stay active
  • Maintain a varied routine to stimulate energy and enthusiasm
  • Wake up early and get moving
