Understanding Pitta Prakriti


Pitta is the energy associated with transformation and is one of the three fundamental energies (or doshas) in Ayurveda. Here are some key characteristics and recommendations for individuals with a dominant Pitta dosha:

Characteristics:

  • Medium build
  • strong metabolism
  • sharp intellect

Common Issues:

  • Sensitive skin
  • prone to rashes or inflammation
  • May experience anger or irritability when out of balance

Digestive Patterns:

  • Strong appetite and digestion
  • May have acidic or burning sensations

Diet Recommendations:

  • Warm, moist, and grounding foods
  • Avoid spicy and acidic foods
  • Include plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables

Balance Tips:

  • Stay cool
  • Avoid excessive heat exposure
  • Engage in calming activities like meditation and yoga
